Additional Registration Requirements for Foreign Students

All Foreign Returning and New Students, Diploma, Undergraduate, and Postgraduate students must satisfy the following registration requirements before they can proceed with there studies in the Unviersity:

Possession of the following documents

  1. Admission Letter
  2. International Passport or ECOWAS Travel Certificate
  3. Valid VISA - STR(Subject to Regularization)
  4. Residence Permit

Failure to secure such documents will lead to suspension of all academic activities

School Leaving

Each and every program a student is admitted into is having its own requirements before a student can legally be seen out. after certifying all those requirements, the next requirement is the school's general requirement that each student most undergo a clearance stage. Each student most be cleared from certain departments/offices before s/he can be given a school leaving certificate.

Every student that certify the above conditions shall click the button below to get access to the school clearance portal.Note: Only your various departments have the authority to add qualified students to the clearance portal. Whoever couldn't access the portal after qualifying for the requirements shall contact his/her department.
